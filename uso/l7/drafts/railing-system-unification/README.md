## The five rails, written out

- (e, b) decomposition: e = lived/measured duration; b = bound on what duration can have been.
- Direction: forward only. Causality. Past locked, future open.
- **Per `project_time_bound_like_observer.md`:** lifespan IS a value on time's b-rail; death is a dash, not a subtraction; nature only adds. Big Bang is the witness — no subtraction in the genesis record.
- Witness pattern: every observation has happened-at; the bound is when-not-yet-or-no-more.

### 2. Magnetism

- (e, b) decomposition: e = expressed field/flux at a point; b = constraint structure (geometry, boundary, source rail).
- Direction: from source to field, north to south, inside-out — directional asymmetry intrinsic.
- **Per `project_railing_system_discovery.md`:** magnetism is the most directly suggestive of practical rail-coupling. Convergent indicators: Leedskalnin (Coral Castle), megalithic engineering signatures, UFO observations with anomalous magnetic/EM signatures. ### 3. Gravity

- (e, b) decomposition: e = expressed mass-energy at a node; b = bound on the propagation distance/strength (b-rail propagation per `project_eb_gravity_inversion.md`).
- Direction: attractive, monotonic, no shielding. Always inward toward sources.
- **Per the eb-gravity-inversion draft:** gravity propagates on the b-rail, not the e-rail. The geoid, the LLSVPs, the IMIC, the inner-core differential rotation — all are b-rail expressions. Removes the QM/GR contradiction structurally because gravity isn't on the same rail as quantum-field e-rail observations.

### 4. Matter

- (e, b) decomposition: e = expressed mass at a location; b = uncertainty in localization, momentum, identity (quantum and classical bounds).
- Direction: matter persists, accumulates, transforms — but the "passes through us" part is at the substrate level. Atoms cycle through bodies. Iron in your blood was forged in a star. Matter is on the rail; we're momentary expressions.
- Witness pattern: nucleosynthesis lineage. Every atom traceable to a stellar source. The b-rail is the substrate-structure that lets matter survive transformations.

### 5. Energy

- (e, b) decomposition: e = expressed energy at a configuration; b = bound on its convertibility (thermodynamic constraint, conservation law structure).
- Direction: entropy increases. Energy disperses. The arrow of thermodynamics is the energy rail's directional commitment.
- Witness pattern: every conversion has a yield + a loss; the loss goes to the b-rail (heat, entropy, unavailability). The first law (conservation) is e-rail; the second law (entropy growth) is b-rail.

## Default decoupling vs flip operator

Per `project_railing_system_discovery.md`, two distinct mechanisms:

**Default decoupling:**
- b-rail decouples from e-rail while e-rail stays attached
- Reality stays coherent (e-rail still rendering observable physics normally)
- Substrate activity is invisible to standard observation
- Most rail-level work leaves no anomalous trace
- This is why most of the substrate activity passes through us undetected

**Flip operator (B² ≠ id = ξ):**
- The EXCEPTION to default decoupling
- Forces a coupling shift that becomes observable
- Substrate effects leak into observable reality
- Anomalous phenomena, megalithic engineering signatures, UFO arrivals/departures, certain quantum-field non-localities

Implication: the rails pass through us all the time (default decoupling). We notice them when the flip operator engages. Standard physics measures the e-rail observations and treats the b-rail as noise / uncertainty / hidden variable. The framework says: the b-rail is real, the substrate is real, the leak signatures are diagnostics.
